Geographical Location of Japan

Japan is an island nation located in East Asia, situated in the Pacific Ocean. It lies to the east of the Korean Peninsula and China, and to the southeast of Russia, separated by the Sea of Japan. Japan consists of four main islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—and thousands of smaller islands, forming an archipelago. Known for its mountainous terrain and active volcanoes, including Mount Fuji, Japan stretches across several climate zones, from the snowy regions of Hokkaido to the subtropical areas of Okinawa.
